Art story about Aerial Silks

Aerial silks , the elegant and dynamic performance art where acrobats execute feats of strength and flexibility
while suspended from fabric, has a history as rich and layered as the material itself

Tracing its origins back to a French circus school in the late 1950s, aerial silks began not just as an act of
entertainment but as a discipline that challenged the conventions of traditional circus arts.
